Why Class 10 Students Struggle in Boards

Parents, Class 10 CBSE boards are a turning point. Here's why many students underperform.

📝 Weak Foundation from Class 9

Class 10 builds on Class 9 concepts. If basics in Algebra, Geometry, or Physics weren't clear, Class 10 becomes overwhelming.

😰 Exam Pattern Confusion

CBSE board exam pattern is different from school tests. Students practice wrong question types and waste time on low-weightage topics.

⏰ Poor Time Management

SST, Science, Maths, English, Hindi – 5 subjects to cover. No study plan means last-minute panic and incomplete syllabus.

😔 Maths & Science Fear

Trigonometry, Quadratic Equations, Carbon Compounds – these chapters intimidate students. They avoid practice and lose marks.
